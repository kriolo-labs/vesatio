# VESATIO MANIFESTO: THE LAW OF SILENT WEALTH

## 0. THE VISION
Vesatio is not a construction company. It is an **Operating System for Ultra-Luxury Real Estate**.  
We build monuments of silence for the elite.  
Our software must reflect this: **Dark. Architectural. Silent.**

## 1. VISUAL DNA (THE BLACK DIAMOND)
- **Primary Vibe**: "Dark Luxury", "Architectural", "Silent Wealth".
- **Color Palette**:
    - **Base**: Deep Onyx Black (`#050505`) & Dark Slate (`#0F0F0F`).
    - **Accent**: Metallic Gold Gradient (`linear-gradient(135deg, #BF953F, #FCF6BA, #B38728)`). 
    - **Highlight**: Diamond White (`#E6E6E6`).
- **Textures**:
    - Backgrounds: Black Marble / Dark Velvet.
    - Borders: Glowing Gold Gradient.

## 2. TYPOGRAPHY
- **Headings**: `Cinzel Decorative` (The "Vesatio" Logo font).
- **Body**: `Manrope` or `Inter` (The "Exclusive Innovations" tagline).

## 3. SITE STRUCTURE
